About US

History & Location

St. Triza Mundere Girls High is located in Budalangi Constituency, Busia County along port victoria high-way. It is a reputable boarding and day secondary school and has gained recognition for its commitment to empowering young girls through quality education and holistic development.

Our Vision

To nurture responsible, empowered, and successful women in society.

Our Mission

Providing quality education, discipline, and holistic development
